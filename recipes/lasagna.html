Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic, salt, and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  2. Brown the ground beef with garlic, salt, and pepper, then mix in tomato sauce.
  3. Layer the noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, mozzarella, and parmesan cheese in a baking dish.
  4. Repeat the layers and top with cheese.
  5. Bake at 375Â°F (190Â°C) for 45 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  6. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
